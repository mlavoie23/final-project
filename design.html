Temporal Artisan Ales

Temporal Artisan Ales is an independent brewery operating out of Vancouver, BC. Established in 2018 by, Tristan Stewart, this brewery offers funky sours & heavy, malt-forward ales. Beyond this logo, I also paint watercolours for all of their logos as well as take product shots for their instagram. Learn more about Temporal here.

Thiessen Farms

Thiessen Farms grows over 400 varieties of at least 40 different vegetables plus herbs, edible flowers and cut sunflowers in Jordan Station, Ontario. This is a family business, established in 1948 & currently being run by 3rd generation farmer, Amy Thiessen. For this project, there were were looking for a logo that was natural & clean.
